Privacy Policy

Quick Note — by ofb media

Effective Date: November 2025

1. Information Collection and Use

Quick Note does not collect any personal information from users without explicit consent. The app may ask for permissions (such as storage or microphone access) purely to enable app features like saving notes or using voice input. No data is transmitted to our servers.

2. Permissions Used

  • Storage: Used to store your notes locally on your device.
  • Microphone: Used for converting speech into text during note creation.
  • Internet: Only used when you enable cloud backup or ads.

3. Data Security

Your notes and information are stored securely within your device. If you use Google Drive backup, your data is protected by Google’s security systems. We never collect, sell, or share user data.

4. Third-Party Services

Quick Note may use third-party SDKs such as Google AdMob (for ads) or Firebase (for analytics). These services may collect anonymous usage statistics in compliance with their respective privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Quick Note is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

6. Policy Updates

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
